DucoFlow is an integration and workflow platform used by authorized teams to connect and manage social media accounts across client workspaces.

It supports secure account authorization, profile identification, analytics visibility, content listing, and future workflow automation across platforms such as TikTok, Meta, LinkedIn, YouTube, blogs, and other digital channels.

What DucoFlow does

  • Connects authorized social media accounts using official APIs
  • Retrieves public profile details such as username, avatar, bio, and profile link
  • Displays account metrics for analysis and reporting
  • Lists authorized public videos and content assets
  • Supports future workflow automation for internal authorized teams

Current use

DucoFlow is currently being used as an internal operational platform to support integrations between official social media APIs and Duco.

Its initial purpose is to facilitate secure connectivity, account management, visibility of authorized assets, analytics support, and workflow enablement for client workspaces.

TikTok Login

Authorized users can sign in with TikTok to securely connect their account and grant access to approved DucoFlow workspace features.

This Login Kit flow is used to identify the authorized account, retrieve public profile information, display account metrics, and list authorized public videos.
