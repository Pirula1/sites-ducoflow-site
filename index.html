What DucoFlow does

DucoFlow provides the infrastructure layer used to connect authorized social accounts and organize trusted data visibility inside client workspaces.

  • Connects authorized social media accounts using official APIs
  • Retrieves public profile details such as username, avatar, bio, and profile link
  • Displays account metrics for analysis and reporting
  • Lists authorized public videos and content assets
  • Supports future workflow automation for internal authorized teams

Current use

DucoFlow is currently being used as an internal operational platform to support integrations between official social media APIs and Duco.

Its initial purpose is to facilitate secure connectivity, account management, visibility of authorized assets, analytics support, and workflow enablement for client workspaces.
