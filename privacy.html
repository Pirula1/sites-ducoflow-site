Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

DucoFlow is an integration and workflow platform used by authorized teams to connect and manage social media accounts across client workspaces.

Information We Access

With user authorization and through official platform APIs, DucoFlow may access account information such as public profile details, profile metadata, authorized account metrics, and authorized public content lists.

How We Use Information

We use authorized data to:

  • identify and display connected social accounts
  • show profile details such as username, avatar, bio, and profile link
  • display analytics and reporting information
  • support internal workflow operations for authorized teams

Data Sharing

DucoFlow does not sell personal data. Data is used only for authorized operational, analytics, and workflow purposes within the relevant client workspace and internal platform environment.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to support authorized integrations, workspace operations, compliance, and legitimate business purposes.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect authorized account data and access credentials.

User Rights

Authorized users may request disconnection of their account or removal of stored integration-related data, subject to legal, contractual, and operational obligations.
